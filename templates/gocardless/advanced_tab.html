Connect with GoCardless
What is GoCardless?

GoCardless is a secure way to connect to your bank accounts. It provides a standardized API that allows Dollar Dollar Bill Y'all to access your financial data without storing your bank credentials.

GoCardless never shares your bank credentials with us. We only receive the transaction data you authorize.
How to Connect
  1. Click the "Get GoCardless Secret Tokens" button below
  2. You'll be redirected to GoCardless to create a new account
  3. Retrieve the secret id and secret key from your user from this link
  4. Paste the secret id and secret key in their respective fields
  5. Click "Connect" to retrieve an access token
  6. Select your bank in the selection menu
